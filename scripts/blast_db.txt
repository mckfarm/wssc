Make a blastdb to compare Dechloromonas sequences with known Dechloromonas PAO sequences

module load blast/2.12.0

Phosphorivorans -----

cd /projects/b1052/mckenna/blast_db/dechloro_vans
makeblastdb -in ref_phosphorivorans.fasta -parse_seqids -dbtype nucl -out vans

blastn -query /projects/p31629/wssc/blast_dechloro/seqs_dechloro.fasta \
-db vans -task blastn -evalue 1e-10 \
-out /projects/p31629/wssc/blast_dechloro/blast_vans.txt -outfmt 6
